About Us

Demetrie Luke's passion for working in education led to a passion in working with local community leaders and businesses to enrich their businesses and the communities they represent. He created this company initially to provide support services aimed at unlocking the potential of businesses that could use help in their growth. To this day, Demetrie has helped business owners generate more leads and streamline their sales process to generate thousands of extra dollars per month by gathering information about their target niche and helping business owners to scale with paid advertising.

In his own words: "I come across so many people who have great ideas or provide great services but may not have the time, knowledge or team to get their message out to people looking for their services."
